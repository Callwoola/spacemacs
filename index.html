Spacemacs is a proposition of a complete new experience for Emacs focused on

ergonomics, mnemonics and consistency.


Mnemonic

Key bindings

Spacemacs organizes key bindings by mnemonic namespaces as much as possible.

Discoverability

Total control

Spacemacs comes with a dedicated major mode spacemacs-mode. Its goal is to give useful feedbacks and easily perform maintenance tasks.

Consistency

No decision fatigue

Similar functionalities should have the same key binding no matter which major is currently active.

Crowd-Configured

Open source power

By defining an very light structure called configuration layer which is easy to understand, Spacemacs makes it easy to contribute additional support.
